Welcome to the **Spring AI Course** — your gateway to integrating **Artificial Intelligence into Java applications** using the power of Spring Boot.

This guide is designed to give you a **clear starting point**, helping you understand what you’ll learn and how to approach the course effectively. Whether you're new to AI or an experienced Java developer, this course will help you bridge the gap between **backend development and modern AI capabilities**.

---

## **Purpose of This Guide**

Spring AI brings the power of **Generative AI (LLMs)** directly into the Java ecosystem. Instead of switching to other languages, you can now build intelligent features within your existing Spring applications.

This guide will help you:

* Understand how AI integrates with Spring Boot
* Learn the overall structure of AI-powered applications
* Connect concepts like **ChatModel, ChatClient, and APIs**
* Stay focused and confident throughout your learning journey


---

## **What You’ll Explore**

Throughout this course, you will work with:

* **OpenAI & Anthropic** (cloud-based AI models)
* **Ollama** (local AI models running on your machine)
* **Spring AI framework** for seamless integration
* **REST APIs** to communicate with AI models
* **Frontend + Backend flow** for real-world applications

You’ll move from **basic setup → model interaction → advanced integrations**, building a strong practical understanding.

---

## **How to Approach This Course**

To get the most out of your learning:

* Write and test code alongside the lectures
* Experiment with different prompts and models
* Explore both cloud and local AI options
* Focus on understanding the flow, not just syntax

> **Consistency matters more than speed.**

---

## **Ready to Begin?**

You are about to learn how modern applications become **intelligent, interactive, and AI-powered**.

Start simple, stay curious, and enjoy the process of building with **Spring AI**.
